DAVID PAYSDEN

Born in Brighton and raised in Manchester, David Paysden is co-founder and lead songwriter of alt pop band VANARIN, chosen by Glass Beams as opening act for their debut Italian show. As a musician, producer and visual artist, David Paysden turns cultural critique into a pop experience — visual, sonic, and playful — where lightness becomes a form of rebellion. His new live show is a true one-man band performance: an immersive, mesmerizing vintage DJ set built around real-time remixes, live vocals, and guitar, featuring tracks from his latest EP 3D Coffee and previewing new unreleased material coming soon.

Big N Small

Big 'n' Small is an intimate dive into yourself through nostalgia, acceptance, forgiveness and change. No distractions. No judgment. A quiet space where you can finally sit with who you are. In this stillness, honesty becomes easier. The past softens, the future feels gentle, as solitude gives you the time to truly listen to yourself. The highest highs and the smallest details quietly remind you of who you are.

Love it all, big and small.
